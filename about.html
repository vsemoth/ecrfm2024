About Us

Ekhephini Community Radio (E C R) was founded in October 2005 by the community of Barkly East in Joe Gabi District. It was granted a broadcasting licence by the independent Community Authority of South Africa (ICASA) IN 2006

Although located in Barky East, ECR will broadcast within the Joe Gqabi region, covering :

  1. Malitswai Local Municipality - Aliwal North and Jamestown
  2. Elundini Local Municipality _ Maclear , Ugei ,Mount Fletcher end part of tsolo
  3. Gariep Local Municipality _ Burgersdorp , Ventestad and Steynsburg
  4. Senqu Local Municipality _ Barkly East , Sterkspruit , Lady Grey , Rhodes and Rossuow

‘Ekhephini Community Radio is a geographical founded station, with the intention end capacity to broadcast in four languages:

  1. IsiXhosa 70%
  2. Southern Sotho 20%
  3. English 5% end
  4. Afrikaans 5%
